John Gordon entered the spaceship. It wasn't too unusual to find abandoned pirate spaceships near Terranova these days, but this wasn't what bothered him. No. Not at all. It was the strange voice inside his head that he couldn't forget. Maybe the mind plays strange tricks on you when you're half-awake? He thought. Maybe it was the booze he and the boys had yesterday? Maybe it was just a dream? The voice said he is from the year 3522, a bit ironic seeing how John just entered the control room of the spaceship and noticed the year of production written as: "2022". "Hmm close but not close enough?" John thought. The irony of faith, right? Never too close, always too suspicious.
